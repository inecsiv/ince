About Us

At i-NexT Computer Education, headquartered in Sivasagar, Assam, we are dedicated to providing high-quality computer education to empower individuals in our local community and beyond. Our institution stands as a beacon of excellence, offering a comprehensive curriculum tailored to meet the demands of the rapidly evolving field of computer science. With a team of experienced instructors, modern facilities, and a commitment to innovation, we strive to nurture the talents of our students and equip them with the skills, knowledge, and confidence needed to thrive in today's digital world. At i-NexT, we believe in unlocking the full potential of every individual and shaping a brighter future through education.

Our Courses

  • ADCA - Advanced Diploma in Computer Applications

    This advanced course builds upon the foundation of computer applications, delving deeper into programming languages, software development, database management, and advanced topics in IT.

  • DCT - Diploma in Computer Technology

    Provides comprehensive training in computer hardware, networking, and system administration, preparing students for careers in IT support and infrastructure management.

  • PGDCA - Post Graduate Diploma in Computer Applications

    An advanced program covering advanced topics in computer applications, software development methodologies, database management systems, and IT project management.

  • DCA - Diploma in Computer Applications

    A comprehensive course covering fundamental computer skills including office productivity software, programming basics, and an introduction to database management.

  • ACC - Advanced Certificate Course

    An intensive course focusing on advanced topics in computer science and technology, including algorithm design, data structures, and advanced programming languages.

  • CCA - Certificate in Computer Applications

    This course provides essential skills in word processing, spreadsheets, presentations, and basic database management, empowering students for office productivity.

  • CST - Certificate in Software Testing

    Equips students with software quality assurance principles, testing methodologies, and tools for ensuring functionality and reliability of software applications.

  • DCP - Diploma in Cybersecurity and Privacy

    Focuses on cybersecurity fundamentals, ethical hacking techniques, data protection methods, and privacy regulations, preparing students for roles in cybersecurity and data protection.
